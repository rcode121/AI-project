PDF Interrogation

Ever found yourself with a mountain of PDFs and specific questions about the content within? With AIChatBox by Ritesh, you no longer have to shift through endless pages to find your answers. Simply upload your PDF files, and our advanced AI will assist you in extracting the information you need. Whether it's data from a research paper, details from a contract, or insights from a report, our platform brings answers to your fingertips.

How It Works:

  • Ask Questions: Type your specific questions related to the uploaded PDF.
  • Get Answers: Receive accurate and instant answers extracted directly from the document.
